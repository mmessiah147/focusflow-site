References to "we," "us," or "our" in this document refer exclusively to PVLabs LLC.

1. Information We Collect

PVLabs LLC collects only the information necessary to provide the FocusFlow service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you create an account
  • Usage data — focus sessions, tasks completed, session duration, and streak data
  • User-generated content — task names, focus room messages, and saved session templates
  • Profile information — optional fields such as your display name and user type provided during onboarding
  • Premium status — whether your account has been upgraded to FocusFlow Pro
  • Device information — device type, operating system, and browser type for compatibility purposes only

2. How We Use Your Information

PVLabs LLC uses the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FocusFlow service
  • Track your focus streaks, session history, and in-app achievements
  • Display leaderboard rankings within the Community feature
  • Send session reminders and productivity nudges (only if you opt in)
  • Process and verify your Premium subscription status
  • Respond to your support and privacy requests
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service.

3. Data We Collect Automatically

When you use FocusFlow, PVLabs LLC automatically collects limited technical data strictly necessary to operate the service:

  • Session tokens — authentication tokens stored securely to keep you logged in. These expire automatically and are never shared with third parties.
  • Local storage data — your active timer state, draft task lists, selected audio preferences, binaural beat selections, and bell tone settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Focus session records — session duration, tasks completed, and dates are stored server-side to power your streak tracking and leaderboard position.
  • Onboarding preferences — your selected user type is stored to personalize task suggestions.

PVLabs LLC does not collect: location data, contact lists, camera or microphone access, advertising identifiers, financial information, biometric data, or any sensitive personal information beyond what is listed in this policy.

4. Cookies

FocusFlow uses only essential session cookies required for authentication and maintaining your logged-in state. PVLabs LLC does not use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Third-party analytics cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Any cookies that persist beyond functional necessity

You may disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so will prevent you from remaining logged in to FocusFlow but will not otherwise affect your ability to access the site.

5. Data Sharing

PVLabs LLC does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share information only in these strictly limited circumstances:

  • Infrastructure provider — we use Base44 as our backend platform. Your data is stored securely on Base44's servers. See Base44's privacy policy.
  • Community features — your display name and focus minutes are visible to other users in the Leaderboard. Focus room messages are visible to room participants. All community participation is voluntary.
  • Legal obligations — PVLabs LLC may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of PVLabs LLC, its users, or the public.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Base44's secure infrastructure. PVLabs LLC implements reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active. Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

FocusFlow is not directed at children under the age of 13. PVLabs LLC does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact us at privacy@pvlabs.us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data PVLabs LLC holds about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — opt out of any marketing communications at any time

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact PVLabs LLC at privacy@pvlabs.us. We will respond within 30 days.
