How it works

Three steps to your
most productive session.

  1. 01

    Build your session

    Add tasks with time estimates from the 120+ library, type your own, or let the AI coach suggest. Drag to reorder. Save your wheel for next time.

  2. 02

    Run the Focus Wheel

    Your tasks become a spinning wheel with the timer inside. A bell sounds when each task is done. Background persistence keeps it running if you switch apps.

  3. 03

    Track your flow

    Session history, streak tracking, focus minutes and achievements build over time. See how you rank on the community leaderboard and invite friends to focus rooms.
